Dent Repair

Specialized dent repair services to restore your vehicle's smooth, flawless exterior

Expert Dent Repair Services

At Auto Touch Ups, we offer professional dent repair solutions for vehicles of all makes and models. Our skilled technicians are trained in both traditional dent repair methods and Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) techniques to address dents of various sizes and severity.

We can effectively remove dents caused by hail damage, door dings, minor collisions, and other impacts, restoring your vehicle's appearance without affecting the factory finish.

Paintless Dent Repair for minimal intervention

Traditional dent repair for more severe damage

Specialized hail damage repair

Preservation of factory paint finish when possible
