About auto touch ups

Auto Touch Ups is a family-owned collision repair shop that has been serving Calgary since 2003. We specialize in comprehensive automotive body repair services including bumper repair, frame straightening, dent removal, and professional paint matching. Our reputation is built on quality craftsmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Our team of certified technicians uses the latest technology and techniques to restore your vehicle to its pre-accident condition. We work with all insurance companies and provide free estimates for all repairs. At Auto Touch Ups, we understand that accidents happen, and we're committed to making the repair process as stress-free as possible. We take pride in our work and stand behind every repair with our satisfaction guarantee.
